Katniss Everdeen

Born to a coal miner and a healer from the poorest of the distructs in Panem , Katniss Everdeen won hearts across the nation by her display of love for her sister, even facing certain death in the Hunger Games so as to spare her sister. She then shot to fame with her stellar archery and her undying love for her fellow district tribute Peeta Mellark, by nursing him back from the brink of death, and even fighting to the death to procure medicine for him. She was crowned winner of the Hunger games alongside Peeta, amidsta a controversial final stand wherein both threatened to end their lives, rather than fight each other.
